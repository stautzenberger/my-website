Welcome to my site! — an introduction

Hi, I’m Sheikyon! Welcome to sheikyon.me, I’m a full-stack developer (software developer) enthusiastic about computing, programming, electronics and free and open-source software. In addition to everything that is even slightly close to computers, I also have a deep interest in mathematics, economics, philosophy, sociology, history, and anime. If you take a closer look at this space, you’ll realize that these are the main topics that are usually addressed very regularly in my articles/posts on my website.

I maintain this website to disclose things that are significant to me and important to common knowledge. In general, my articles tend to be detailed in terms of content and in some cases they can be considerably long — I care about the logic and semantic consistency behind his prose, so I always try to correct any inconsistencies that may lead to misunderstandings or undesirable errors. My articles are usually long and the topic on which they revolve is linked to my personal hobbies, such as those already mentioned above.

(Writing and divulgation is not for me an obligation that I do to earn money, but rather a hobby, rather a simple passion to which I can dedicate hours and even days, when it comes to considerably longer writings.)

When it comes to mathematics, I feel a particular attraction to algebra, differential and integral calculus, number theory + analytical number theory, probability/statistics, and logic. In quite a few of my articles I try to find a new perspective on a subject so that I can get to analyze it from either philosophy or economics and even other areas/fields with little apparent connection.

Since I have other things to do besides dedicating my time to writing, I will publish about two or three articles a month, often I will do this because I want to stick to a routine. When I’m not writing around here, I’m probably programming, reading a book or literary work, riding my bike somewhere not too far from home, talking with friends, or wasting my time on some non-productive leisure activity.

To this day, I live in Gran Canaria, Spain (UTC+00:00), Spanish being my birth language, although near my pre-adolescence I managed to begin to develop with English with quite agility. In short, I speak English, German and some Italian but I am trying hard to defend myself in other languages such as French or Greek.

I consider myself especially skilled at moving around with Python, JavaScript, TypeScript + Node.js, Ruby, Fortran, CSS + SCSS/SASS and HTML. Not surprisingly, I am currently learning C++, PHP, Bash/Shell, R and Rust. I also handle technologies like MathJax which I usually use to facilitate the process of visualizing mathematical formulas expressed in LaTeX, due to its high typographic quality, versatility and extensiveness.

Hardware and software

I bought not many months ago, an Alienware x17 R2 laptop to which I installed EndeavourOS with KDE Plasma desktop environment. Initially I planned to use it for professional use, such as multimedia editing, but ended up becoming disenchanted with it — I use it primarily for photo editing, programming, gaming, office automation, and other activities that involve leisure or learning.

I don’t think it hurts to say that I have a not very old Compaq Presario C700 which, despite its characteristics, today performs excellently for some of my demands. I have tried to expand its RAM memory from 2GB to 4GB, occupying the only free slot it had previously, it has Linux Mint installed as the only operating system with Cinnamon as the desktop environment. It remains to consider my IBM Thinkpad T43 which I am fond of for its aesthetics and its optimal performance with Linux distros such as Void Linux.
